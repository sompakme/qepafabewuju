The Truth About Malaysia Pargo's Divorce

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Divorce is never easy, but Malaysia Pargo handled her separation from Jannero Pargo with class. In an interview with Rolling Out, Malaysia got candid about her divorce and made it clear that her children's well-being is her top priority. "After divorce, it's not about you. It's about the kids," she explained to the publication, adding that she wanted to ensure that her kids remained "whole." Malaysia also got candid about co-parenting, saying that it's "the most important thing because, first off, you broke a home, and kids realize it." The reality star noted that she and Pargo "co-parent well" and that, despite their divorce, they can "stay in the same household" to be there for their kids.

Since her divorce from Pargo, Malaysia has focused on her children, reality TV career, and jewelry line. Though she hasn't divulged too many details about the cause of her divorce, we'll probably get some details in the upcoming season of Basketball Wives.
