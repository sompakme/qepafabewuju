10 Effortless All-White Outfit Formulas for Classic Summer Style

Publish date: 2024-09-19

A summer capsule wardrobe isn’t complete without an assortment of airy pieces you can combine into several all-white outfits to carry you through the warm weather season. Gauzy pieces in creams and ivory offer effortless dressing solutions amid sweat-inducing temperatures. There's also something about a monochromatic look that’s synonymous with looking instantly pulled together and the reason why the fashion set fills their closets with all-white outfits to wear throughout summer. Mastering the white-on-white look also offers an easy dressing formula for wearing white after Labor Day—despite what naysayers say about the unwritten fashion rule.

To help achieve that crisp white outfit (or a breathable, airy linen version), we turned to one of the best sources of inspiration—street style. From timeless suit sets to separates—like a polo top paired with a pleated skirt (very on theme with the tennis dressing aesthetic of the moment) to drop-waist summer dresses and tailored vests styled with pencil skirts, there's endless mix-and-match potential.

Ahead, shop 10 all-white outfits to pin to your style mood board.
