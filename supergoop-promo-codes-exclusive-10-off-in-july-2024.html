 FAQs 

Does Supergoop offer free shipping?

Supergoop does offer free shipping on orders of $65 or more. When you reach the higher tiers of their rewards program, you can also qualify for free shipping on your orders. Occasionally, we might have a free shipping promo code on this page too.  

Can I get a Supergoop welcome offer?

New customers can receive a 10% Supergoop discount code welcome offer to use on their next order. It's a great way to make some savings if you haven't shopped with Supergoop before. 

When does Supergoop have sales?

One of the best times to shop for Supergoop sunscreen is during their seasonal sales, such as Black Friday. Last year, there were discounts of up to 20% off selected skincare favorites. Whilst this is a smaller discount up to other brands, it’s still one of the best times to save at Supergoop.  

Can I get a student discount at Supergoop?

Sadly, Supergoop does not offer any student discounts at this moment. However, you can still save on your sunscreen and skincare orders as a student with a promo code from our page. Signing up for the rewards program is also a great way to get regular promotions and coupons.  

Does Supergoop offer returns?

Supergoop offers returns within 30 days of purchase, except on final sale products. Just make sure that the item is in unused condition to be eligible for a return or exchange.
