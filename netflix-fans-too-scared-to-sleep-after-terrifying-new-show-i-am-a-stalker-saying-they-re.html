Netflix fans too scared to sleep after terrifying new show I Am A Stalker saying theyre

Publish date: 2024-07-16

NETFLIX fans have confessed they are "too scared to sleep" after watching new horror I Am A Stalker.

The documentary, originally released last year, sees convicted stalkers open up about their crimes.

The Netflix show depicts in eerie detail how the criminals' love turned into deep obsession.

The streaming service warns viewers of "harrowing accounts of harassment, abuse and other crimes" from both stalkers and their survivors.

In the I Am A Stalker content, it states: "Roughly 1.4 million people are stalked every year in the US alone.

"The results of stalking range from the annoying (persistently receiving loads of email or letters) to the intrusive (being secretly watched or followed) to the most severe (kidnapping and/or murder).”

It seems fans have been left hugely troubled by the candid scenes, with one confessing: "I certainly can’t be the only one who watches #IAmAStalker on #Netflix before bed, and therefore will be too scared to sleep?"

A second mused: "Hearing how the judicial system continued to fail these victims is [crazy Emoji icon]. A piece of paper doesn’t protect anyone. Thankful I am single at this point."

A third referenced one case and wrote: "I feel for John, but he put Rachel and her family through absolute hell. I'd be terrified to be anywhere near him after that. I can only imagine what would happen if Rachel met someone and moved on."

Another confessed: "I'm watching #IAmAStalker and it has me scared."

One wailed: "I’m going to have nightmares tonight #IAmAStalker is so scary OMG!" while another added: "Other than that this show induces nightmares."

Daniel Thompson is one of the main characters of Netflix's new series, I Am a Stalker.

Daniel is currently serving a life sentence in Missouri for second-degree murder.

Prior to this life sentence, Daniel kept going after his wife Angie, despite a restraining order, following the breakdown of their marriage.

He initially broke into Angie's house after she returned from the domestic violence shelter with their children to steal. He got caught and was convicted of burglary.

After serving seven years, he began the same pattern of stalking with his new girlfriend and subsequently got probation.

With the next woman he dated, he went to her home to kill her and also stabbed a man to death.

Recently, Netflix viewers also turned off show His House, saying it "scared the hell" out of them.

They also confessed they were "stressed and unable to sleep" after another particular crime series.
