Armor - Wild Hearts Guide

Publish date: 2024-09-16

There are two main types of modifications: The human path and the Kemono path. The human path is for those who like to use human technology to give them an edge during combat, while the Kemono path is for those who understand the kemono and want to be one with nature. Depending on the path you choose, you'll be able to activate different skills for your armor.

Not all armor pieces will be available for modifications. Moreover, you'll need to craft first the base version of the armor you want to modify and gather special materials, like teardrops or blood, to craft these modifications.

Once you have all the required materials for a modification, go to the Armor menu in the forge, then to the Human or Kemono path tabs, and select the modification you want.
