Vince McMahons Errol Flynn-Like Mustache Wins Over Eric Bischoff

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Vince McMahon ruled WWE with an iron fist for decades, as every single decision went through him, down to the smallest details. McMahon is also someone who is never afraid of changes, no matter what anyone else says. This includes his new look featuring a mustache for the first time in his life. Now it appears Eric Bischoff is a big fan of McMahon’s mustache.

Vince McMahon is truly larger than life and there are simply far too many peculiarities about him that make people wonder whether they are very real or not. Fans were utterly shocked when McMahon started sporting a mustache earlier this year but have since gotten used to it.

While speaking on his 83 Weeks podcast, WWE Hall of Famer Eric Bischoff talked about Vince McMahon’s new look. Bischoff made it clear he is a big fan of the look and stated that McMahon looked like golden age Hollywood actor Errol Flynn.

”I kind of dug it. It put a smile on my face. The biggest thing I noticed is Vince wasn’t wearing a tie. He’s dying his hair, and he’s dying his mustache. He looks great. I talked to someone recently who said he’s down in the gym working out, you know? In fact, the comment was, ‘If you didn’t know he had just gone through back surgery. You would never have imagined it,’ and that’s Vince McMahon.

Vince, once again, he looked dapper as f*** in his lavender sport coat, sporting that Errol Flynn mustache.”

Ringside News exclusively reported that Vince McMahon started changing creative week after his spinal surgery. He also returned to the gym after spinal surgery. Regardless, we’ll have to wait and see how McMahon will continue to control WWE in the coming weeks.
