Creating a Normal Probability Plot is a simple process.

Arrange your x-values in ascending order by clicking on them.

Calculate fi = (i-0.375)/(n+0.25), where I denotes the position of the data value in the ordered list and n is the number of observations in the ordered list.

Calculate the z-score for each of the fi

Plot your x-values on the horizontal axis, together with the matching z-scores on the vertical axis.
