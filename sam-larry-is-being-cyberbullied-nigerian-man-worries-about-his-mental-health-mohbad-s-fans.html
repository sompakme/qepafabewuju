Sam Larry Is Being Cyberbullied: Nigerian Man Worries About His Mental Health, Mohbads Fans

Publish date: 2024-08-10
  • Nigerian socialite Sam Larry is trending on social media over claims of him facing cyberbullying
  • This all started after a netizen noted that the public figure’s social media comment section was always in shambles
  • The cyberbullying claims about Sam Larry triggered a series of reactions from Mohbad’s fans as they recounted what he did to the late singer

Singer Azeez Fashola, aka Naira Marley’s associate, Sam Larry, has become a topic of discussion on social media after a netizen raised concerns about his mental health.

It is no longer news that after Mohbad’s death, Sam Larry, with the real name Balogun Olamilekan Eletu, was one of those made scapegoats by Nigerians for being responsible. Many made it a point to camp in his comment section to rain curses on him and his family.

Chike pays tribute to Mohbad at AMVCA

In other news, Legit.ng reported that Nigerian singer Chike attended the recently held African Magic Viewers Choice Awards (AMVCA) 2024. He paid tribute to the late Oladimeji Ilerioluwa Aloba, aka Mohbad.

Chike was one of the artistes who performed at the event on Saturday, May 11.

In a video posted on Instagram by @notjustok and spotted by Legit.ng, Chike was spotted performing his hit song with Mohbad, titled Egwu, with his group of dancers on the stage.
