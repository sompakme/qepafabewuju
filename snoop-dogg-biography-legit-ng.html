Snoop Dogg (real name Calvin Cordozar Broadus, Jr.) was born in the Californian city of Long Beach on October 20, 1971. His father was a musician and a singer. The future artiste began calling himself Snoop Doggy Dogg, when he was a child. It came from his nickname, 'Snoopy.' Snoop's mother gave him this nickname because of his love of the cartoon film of the same name and his excessive curiosity.
