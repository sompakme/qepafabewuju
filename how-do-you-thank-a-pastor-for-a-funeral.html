What is the significance of Pastor Appreciation Month in October?

Annually, on the second Sunday in October, a non-official holiday known as Pastor Appreciation Day is observed by Christians throughout. This event, which is also known as Clergy Appreciation Day, is observed during Pastor Appreciation Month and recognises the accomplishments of priests, pastors, reverends, and ministers across the United States.
