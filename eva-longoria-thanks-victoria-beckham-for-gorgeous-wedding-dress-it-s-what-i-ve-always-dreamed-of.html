Eva Longoria thanks Victoria Beckham for gorgeous wedding dress: 'It's what I've always dreamed of'

Publish date: 2024-08-27

    Following her beautiful wedding earlier this month, Eva Longoria Baston has thanked best friend Victoria Beckham for the "dream" dress she wore on the big day. Eva, 41, tied the knot with Jose 'Pepe' Antonio Baston in an intimate ceremony at their home, with family and friends present.

    Actress Eva wore a breathtaking custom Victoria Beckham gown, and she took to Instagram this week to publicly thank the former Spice Girl. She wrote: "This was the wedding dress I dreamed of! Thank u @victoriabeckham for having the best vision for my dress! Love you!"

    EXCLUSIVE: HELLO! goes inside Eva Longoria and Pepe Baston's stunning wedding celebrations

    Eva Longoria chose Victoria Beckham to design her wedding dress

    Victoria, 42, also shared some behind-the-scenes photos from Eva's magical day, and added: "@evalongoria the most beautiful bride. I am honoured to have created your wedding dress. Thank you for asking me!!! X we love u! X"

    In an exclusive interview with HELLO! Magazine, star Eva opened up about choosing Victoria to create her dress. She explained: "I asked Victoria to do my dress because I wanted it to be special and made with love – and I knew Victoria would make it with love.

    She knew close friend Victoria would "make it with love"

    "Victoria was one of my witnesses, too – she signed the paper that says I was here – so it was a Victoria Beckham weekend. She was the best, sweetest person to have there on this day. Not only did she do my wedding dress but she's one of my best friends so for her to be there was so special."

    It was a star-studded affair attended by stars including Victoria and husband David, Melanie Griffith and Ricky Martin. Eva's former co-star Vanessa Williams also made a surprise appearance to serenade Eva and Jose with her hit song .
