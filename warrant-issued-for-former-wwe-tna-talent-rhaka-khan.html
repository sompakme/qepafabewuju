Warrant Issued for Former WWE &amp; TNA Talent Rhaka Khan

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Rhaka Khan, who was a former developmental talent for both WWE & TNA, has now become subject to a warrant according to court records. Khan fought under the ring name Trenesha Biggers.

The warrant against Biggers comes before her scheduled trial on the custody of her children and facilitating an aggravated kidnapping. The trial is scheduled for Monday, October 4 in El Paso, Texas.

Biggers’ trial has been delayed many times as the pandemic took the legal system in the entire world by a storm. There’s a bench warrant against the former WWE/TNA talent, which is issued when one is an absentee from an older hearing on their cases.

The warrant was listed for Khan on Thursday, September 23. There is another motion hearing under her name for October 12, though it remains unconfirmed what it is for.
