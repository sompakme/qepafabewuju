The 10 best dressed at the Golden Globes 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Greta Lee in Loewe

Michael Tran//Getty Images

Always a match made in sartorial heaven, Greta Lee and Loewe delivered one of our favourite looks of the night. The actress wore a custom cream draped dress from the house which featured a low back and tie detail. She finished her look with Tiffany & Co jewellery.

Elizabeth Olsen in Vivienne Westwood

Michael Tran//Getty Images

Also opting for a bridal-inspired look was Elizabeth Olsen, who looked beautiful in a lace panelled gown by Vivienne Westwood, which – of course – included a perfect corset. She accessorised with jewellery by Reza, and a bold red lip.

Jennifer Aniston in Dolce & Gabbana

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

Jennifer Aniston kept things classic in a strapless black gown by Dolce & Gabbana, which had beaded scalloped detail on the skirt.

Hunter Schafer in Prada

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Hunter Schafer's sensational Prada dress was made for the red carpet – and really came to life as she moved. The pale pink design consisted of a slip dress that was covered in delicate sheer material which trailed behind her as she walked.

Carey Mulligan in vintage Schiaparelli

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

Making a statement with silhouette was Carey Mulligan, who chose a vintage Schiaparelli gown from 1949. The black fishtail dress had additional drama in the form of an embellished bust. Bulgari jewellery completed the look.

Rosamund Pike in Dior

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Rosamund Pike's headdress was one of the highlights of the red carpet. The actress paired her black lace Dior gown with a rather dramatic veil by Philip Treacy in the same fabric, and Boucheron jewels.

Elle Fanning in vintage Balmain

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

Another vintage favourite came courtesy of Elle Fanning who chose a cream silky number from Balmain's archive, which was adorned with a beautiful bow. She paired her gown with classic Cartier diamonds.

Natalie Portman in Dior

Monica Schipper/GA//Getty Images

Natalie Portman delivered serious sparkle with her Dior ballgown, which was completely covered in sequins, and De Beers jewellery.

Brie Larson in Prada

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Another unique look came from Brie Larson, who chose a purple ankle-length design by Prada for the big night. She accessorised with matching pointy pumps and Old Hollywood waves and jewellery by Fred Leighton and Kwiat Diamonds.

Emma Stone in Louis Vuitton

Gilbert Flores/Golden Globes 2024//Getty Images

Emma Stone was a big winner on the night, and she was certainly dressed the part. The actress wore a low-cut embellished evening gown by Louis Vuitton with a dramatic slit up the leg.
