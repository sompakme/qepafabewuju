Nigerian Lady Teaching English in China Receives Heartwarming Xmas Messages from Her Students

Publish date: 2024-09-25
  • A Nigerian teacher was surprised to receive heartwarming Christmas messages from her students who went all out to wish her a warm holidays
  • The early Christmas messages from the children warmed the spirit of the teacher who equally expressed her love for the students
  • Arijenileola Bolanle Victoria who is based in China expressed joy for her ability to touch the lives of the students who have reciprocated by showering her with kind words

A Nigerian teacher based in China has had her table flooded by many Christmas cards, courtesy of her students. The yuletide is here and it is a season of joy during which colourful greeting cards fly around and this particular teacher had her fair share of it.

The lady identified as Arijenileola Bolanle Victoria was showered with kind words by her students who are apparently proud to have her as their teacher.

Early Christmas message

The messages according to the teacher are rather early, but she welcomed them all the same. She wrote:

"My students said it isn't early to say Merry Christmas. So, Merry Christmas it is!"

Bonlanle who teaches English as a Second Language at Deyang No1 Middle School, Deyang China shared the proud moment on LinkedIn. The delighted teacher wrote:

"Proud teacher moment. I'm so grateful for the opportunity to be a teacher. It helps me guide my students, be their friend and embrace all the ideas to make them better. Being a Middle school teacher is growing me and opening me up to things I never knew I was capable of. I love these students of mine."

We love you Ms Vicky, students warm teacher's hearts

The Christmas cards have lovely messages written in the children's own handwriting. One of the children named Wang Yuyag wrote in one of the cards:
