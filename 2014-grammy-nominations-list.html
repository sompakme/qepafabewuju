2014 Grammy Nominations List

Publish date: 2024-07-23

 Some of music’s biggest stars are coming together for tonight's special “The GRAMMY Nominations Concert Live!!”, which is going down live from the Nokia Theatre in Los Angeles. 

The one-hour concert is being hosted by LL Cool J and will see performances from Macklemore, Katy Perry, Robin Thicke, Miguel, Lorde, and more. The show tonight serves as the first look at this year's Grammys and its nominees.

Along with the taping of the performances, the nominees were also revealed Friday night, and JAY Z led the pack this year with nine nominations, including “Album of the Year” for Magna Carta…Holy Grail. Following Hov's 9 nominations are Macklemore & Ryan Lewis, who continue their historic year by earning 7 nominations along with Kendrick Lamar, Justin Timberlake, and Pharrell. Drake and Daft Punk followed with five nominations, while New Zealand sensation Lorde received four nominations including “Song of the Year.”

Check out the nominees from the Rap and R&B field below, and make sure to tune into CBS on January 26th to see the winners of the 56th Annual Grammy Award Show!
