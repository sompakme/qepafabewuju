Katie Holmes praised for sharing stretch marks picture

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Katie Holmes has been praised for sharing a candid picture of her stretch marks on Instagram.

The actress, who is currently enjoying a fashion resurgence thanks to her winning recent looks, posted two images promoting a glossy magazine photoshoot. The first shows her with her hair slicked back in an androgynous suit, while the second - in black and white (swipe to see) - sees her in the same look, but posing to show her stomach.

Fans were quick to note her stretch marks, and to voice their appreciation for her body-positive post.

"Love how beautiful and natural you are with your body," user @iriagalvin wrote. "You aren’t afraid to show your body exactly how it is."

"So bold!!" @jenfletch agreed. "I love that you’re slightly showing the pregnancy warrior marks. You go girl!!!!"

"Thanks for being real," added @sunkisser.

Gotham//Getty Images

Holmes' post reflects a more candid approach to pregnancy and post-baby reality proven popular among certain celebrities and influencers. Both Kourtney Kardashian and Ashley Graham were praised earlier this year for sharing unedited pictures of their stretch marks.
