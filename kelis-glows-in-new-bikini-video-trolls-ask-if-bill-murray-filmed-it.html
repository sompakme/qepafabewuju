Kelis Glows In New Bikini Video, Trolls Ask If Bill Murray Filmed It

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Even at 43 years old, Kelis continues to bring all the boys to the yard. Her life over the past few years has been something of a rollercoaster. From suddenly losing her husband, Mike Mora, to lashing out at Beyonce last summer over her song being sampled on RENAISSANCE, emotions have been flying high. Recent months have seemingly calmed down for the singer-songwriter. However, earlier this month she practically broke the internet with rumours of a new romance.

Apparently, Kelis has been spending time with Ghostbusters actor Bill Murray, who's nearly 30 years her senior. We haven't seen them packing on the PDA quite yet. Still, the Illinois-born entertainer has been spotted in the crowd of several of his apparent new lover's concerts lately. For her part, Kelis has been fuelling Instagram with plenty of thirst trap content, leading followers to speculate that she's recruited Murray as her cameraman.

Kelis Soaks Up the Sun

On Thursday (June 15) afternoon, the mother of three shared a video of her laying out in the sun wearing a bikini, looking as radiant as ever. "NATURAL GLOW BODY BUTTER is nourishing, healing, firming and smells divine. Organic and fortified with royal jelly. Hand-whipped to perfection, creamy, and smooth," Kelis wrote in the caption, encouraging fans to check out her skincare brand on its website.

While a few people told the recording artist of their plans to purchase, many took the opportunity to crack jokes at her expense. "I attribute all current and future camerawork to one Bill Murray, until further notice," one person wrote. "She done put some Ensure in Bill milkshake, and he doesn't know how to act now," another quipped.
