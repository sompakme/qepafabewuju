Duchess of Cambridge Kate Middleton checked blazer

Publish date: 2024-08-27

The Duchess of Cambridge is an expert when it comes to pulling together a smart, workwear-appropriate outfit. Yesterday, the royal turned to one of her go-to pieces, the classic blazer, for a visit to a parenting support group in London.

The royal met with parents and children from PACT (Parents and Children Together), a community organisation in Southwark that aims to improve the health and development of children and their caretakers — a cause close to the royal's heart. Kate joined members and volunteers during their weekly MumSpace meeting, which acts as a support network for parents raising babies and toddlers.

ALASTAIR GRANT//Getty Images

The royal chose a grey-toned blazer for her appearance - in the Prince of Wales check, in a nod to her father in law - with sharp peaked shoulders. She paired her tailored jacket with an all-black ensemble, including tapered LK Bennett trousers, her go-to block-heel Gianvito Rossi court shoes, and a simple black top. She finished her look with a blue pendant necklace from Astley Clarke, a stainless steel watch by Cartier, pearl drop earrings and a diamond-studded ring by Annoushka.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

While Kate's blazer is believed to be from Catherine Walker, a London-based designer that she has been wearing for years, there are plenty of similar options available to buy right now.

Kate often turns to a blazer for her official appearances, whether in a simple navy, a bolder block colour or a classic check, wearing them with everything from pencil skirts and wide-leg trousers to more casual jeans and trainers.

While there's nothing particularly groundbreaking about the the tailored blazer, Kate does prove its versatility. It's a great, easy-to-style piece that can instantly make an outfit look more polished, and well worth investing in if you're looking to create a timeless capsule wardrobe that works for multiple occasions.
