What are the plotlines of this manhwa?

Lookism is a manhwa series that explores the themes of bullying, prejudice, discrimination, and social justice based on physical appearance. The series follows the life of Daniel Park, a high school student who can switch between two different bodies: one fat and ugly, and the other fit and handsome. He uses his handsome body to live a double life and experience various adventures.

Along the way, he meets different characters who have their own stories and struggles, such as Vasco, a gang leader who protects the weak; Jay, a mysterious and wealthy martial artist; Zack, a former model who suffers from alopecia; Crystal, a talented singer who faces sexism and harassment; and many more. The series also features various subplots and arcs that involve different genres, such as comedy, drama, action, romance, mystery, and horror. Some of the notable arcs are:

  • The Hostel Arc: Daniel and his friends stay at a hostel that is haunted by the spirits of the previous residents, who were killed by a group of assassins called the Workers. Daniel discovers that he inherited a mysterious power from the previous owner of his chubby body, Eun Tae, which allows him to summon the spirits of the hostel’s residents and use their abilities.
  • The Fashion Week Arc: Daniel participates in a fashion week event as a model, where he meets various celebrities and influencers. He also faces the challenges and pressures of the fashion industry, such as competition, jealousy, corruption, and exploitation.
  • The Burn Knuckles Arc: Daniel joins Vasco’s gang, the Burn Knuckles, who fight against other gangs and bullies. He learns more about Vasco’s past and his friendship with Jace, who is Daniel’s handsome body’s original owner. He also encounters the Chairman, a powerful and mysterious figure who is behind many of the conflicts and crimes in the series.
  • The Lookism Arc: Daniel and his friends are invited to a secret island by a mysterious organization called Lookism, which claims to offer them a chance to change their appearance and life. However, they soon realize that Lookism is a sinister cult that brainwashes and experiments on people, and they have to escape from the island and expose Lookism’s true nature.
