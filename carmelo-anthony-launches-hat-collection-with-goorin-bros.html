Carmelo Anthony Launches Hat Collection With Goorin Bros

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Oklahoma City Thunder forward Carmelo Anthony has partnered with San Francisco-based Goorin Bros to launch a signature hat collection which will be available today. Melo is the first professional athlete to release a collaborative collection with the company in their 122-year history, according to WWD.

The hat collection, dubbed "Fresh Greens by Melo x Goorin," includes five unisex models, all of which were named by Carmelo and are made in America from 100% wool. Styles include the "Melo Mint," "Everyday Camo" and "Sage Dust."

“As someone who has admired the craftsmanship of Goorin Bros., teaming up with them was a natural fit,” Anthony said. “This collaboration was a valuable experience — being able to be a part of the design process and bringing my creative vision to life. There’s going to be more headwear to come from this partnership. This is just the beginning.”

The hats will reportedly range in price from $180 (Everyday Camo) to $300 for selections like the "Melo Mint." According to WWD, each hate is lined with green satin and a Melo x Goorin logo, Carmelo's signature and the inscription: “Knowledge of self, wisdom and understanding.”

The Fresh Greens by Melo x Goorin collection is available today exclusively online at the Goorin Bro’s website as well as their three stores located in San Francisco, New York City and Las Vegas.
