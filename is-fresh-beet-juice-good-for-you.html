Are canned beets as good as fresh beets?

Nutrients. With only a few exceptions, canned beets have about the same nutritional value as fresh beets. Fresh beets have double the phosphorus, potassium and folate, but 1 cup of sliced, canned beets has 14 percent of the recommended daily intake of folate and 4 percent of phosphorus and potassium.
