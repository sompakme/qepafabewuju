Jack Harlow's Early Life And The Start of His Career

Jack Harlow was born on March 13, 1998, in Louisville, Kentucky. His passion for music began at an early age when he started writing rap songs at 12 years old. In 2015, Harlow released his first mixtape, The Handsome Harlow, which gained some local attention. He continued to release music, and in 2017, his mixtape Gazebo garnered wider recognition. This was the turning point in his career, leading to his signing with Generation Now and Atlantic Records.

Breakthrough Success And Growing Popularity

In 2020, Jack Harlow's career took off with the release of his hit single "Whats Poppin." The song quickly became a viral sensation, peaking at number two on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. A remix featuring Lil Wayne, DaBaby, and Tory Lanez further boosted the song's popularity. As a result, Harlow's fan base expanded rapidly, and his music streams increased exponentially.

The success of "Whats Poppin" paved the way for Harlow's debut studio album, Thats What They All Say, released in December 2020. The album featured collaborations with notable artists such as Big Sean, Lil Baby, and Chris Brown. It debuted at number five on the Billboard 200 chart, solidifying Harlow's place in the rap industry.
