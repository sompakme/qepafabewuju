Are you planning to visit Turtle Creek Mall in Hattiesburg, Mississippi but wondering what time it opens and closes? Do you want to know if the mall is open on weekends or holidays? Here’s a complete guide to Turtle Creek Mall Hattiesburg MS Hours that will answer all your questions.

What Time Does it Open and Close?

Turtle Creek Mall Hattiesburg MS opens at 10:00 am from Monday to Saturday and closes at 9:00 pm. On Sundays, the mall opens at 12:00 pm and closes at 6:00 pm.

Is it Open on Saturdays and Sundays?

Yes, Turtle Creek Mall Hattiesburg MS is open on both Saturdays and Sundays. The mall opens at 10:00 am on Saturdays and closes at 9:00 pm. On Sundays, the mall opens at 12:00 pm and closes at 6:00 pm.
