Plot and Storyline

I Am The Villain is a webtoon that tells the story of Lucy, a modern-day workaholic who gets transported to the world of her best friend’s novel. The novel is a historical romance that revolves around the Duke of Roven, who is the main villain and the love interest of the heroine. Lucy finds herself in the role of the Duke’s fiancée, who is also the villainess who betrays him and causes his downfall. Lucy has to survive in the novel’s world, while trying to find a way to escape and return to her own world. Along the way, she encounters various characters and events from the novel, and she starts to question her role and identity.

I Am The Villain is a webtoon that combines the genres of fantasy, romance, drama, and comedy. It has a captivating plot, a unique premise, a colorful cast of characters, and a stunning art style. It also has a lot of humor, suspense, action, and romance. It is a webtoon that appeals to a wide range of readers, especially those who enjoy stories about isekai, reverse harem, and villainess.
