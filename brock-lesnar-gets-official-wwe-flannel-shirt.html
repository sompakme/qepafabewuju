Brock Lesnar Gets Official WWE Flannel Shirt

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Brock Lesnar has been the de-facto final boss of WWE for years. The Beast Incarnate has done just about all there is to do as a singles competitor. Brock’s popularity has kept Lesnar in the main event scene for part of three decades. The WWE Universe can’t get enough of Brock.

Lesnar occasionally likes to change up his look. Brock most recently returned with long hair and beard. Eventually he added a sleeveless flannel shirt to his attire.

Brock Lesnar fans can now get their hands on their very own officially licensed “Beast” flannel. The shirt boasts dual front pockets. One of them is adorned with his “Beast” logo. The back of the shirt has a larger version.

“No Brock Lesnar outfit is complete without this “Beast” Flannel shirt. Show everyone you’re the Beast Incarnate with this official flannel shirt!”

Fans can complete their Brock Lesnar outfit for $49.99. The shirt is available on WWE’s official shop. It’s the ultimate gift for the Lesnar fan who has everything else there is to own.

Time will tell if the shirt takes off in popularity. If it does, we could start seeing Brock Lesnar cosplay taking over crowds at Raw. If not, there’s probably a great niche market for fans who want to dress like Brock.
