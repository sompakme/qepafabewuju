Watch George Clooneys Lord Hollywood Kiss the Downton Abbey Ladies

The women of Downton Abbey have a tendency to faint when George Clooney is around.

In the video, he plays Lord George Oceans Gravity, Marquis of Hollywood. Cora Crawley (Elizabeth McGovern) is particularly smitten with him, much to Robert Crawley’s (Hugh Bonneville) chagrin.
