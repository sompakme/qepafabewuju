Early Life and Career Beginnings

Lais Ribeiro was born in Teresina, Piauí, Brazil. She had a humble upbringing and initially had no aspirations of becoming a model. However, after giving birth to her son Alexandre, she was encouraged by a friend to try modeling as a career. She started her modeling career locally in Brazil before moving to New York to pursue international opportunities.

Breakthrough in Modeling

Ribeiro’s big break came when she walked in the 2010 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show. This marked the beginning of her association with the brand, and she was officially named a Victoria’s Secret Angel in 2015. Her work with Victoria’s Secret significantly increased her visibility and earning potential in the industry.

Other Modeling Work

Beyond Victoria’s Secret, Ribeiro has worked with a number of high-profile brands and designers. She has walked the runway for Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Dolce & Gabbana, Versace, and Marc Jacobs, among others. She has also appeared in advertising campaigns for Ralph Lauren, Christian Dior, Tom Ford, and American Eagle.

Net Worth Growth

Ribeiro’s net worth has grown steadily over the years. Her earnings come primarily from her modeling contracts, but she also earns money from endorsements and other business ventures. In 2017, Forbes listed her as one of the highest-paid models in the world.

Endorsements and Partnerships

Ribeiro has leveraged her fame to secure lucrative endorsement deals and partnerships. She has worked with brands like Nike, H&M, and L’Oreal, adding significantly to her income.

Philanthropy
