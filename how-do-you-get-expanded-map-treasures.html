How do you get expanded map treasures?

Publish date: 2024-09-28

To get expanded map treasures in the RE4 Remake, you can purchase the Treasure: Map Expansion as standalone DLC content, as part of the RE4 Remake Deluxe Edition, or as part of the RE4 Extra DLC Pack. Once purchased, 41 exclusive treasures will be automatically added to your map that can only be seen with the Treasure Map: Expansion.

Is the RE4 treasure map expansion worth it?

Yes, the RE4 treasure map expansion is definitely worth it. Some treasures in the game are fairly easy to find, while others are almost impossible to find without a map. The expansion adds 41 exclusive treasures to your map, providing more content and a greater sense of accomplishment when finding them.

How do you get treasure maps in eso?

In Elder Scrolls Online, treasure maps are random drops that can be obtained from monsters or found in various containers throughout the game world. However, it’s important to note that you can only find treasure maps for the specific zone you are currently playing in. Each zone has its own set of treasure maps to collect.

How do you get the treasure in the sanctuary RE4 remake?

To get the treasure in the sanctuary of the RE4 Remake, you need to look for a collapsed pillar to the left before climbing out of the sanctuary on the right side. Crouch under the pillar and reach the altar to take the Illuminados Pendant from the monolith behind it. Additionally, there is a chest on the floor outside the door of the Merchant’s final shop that contains treasure.

Do you need the treasure maps RE4 remake?

The treasure maps in the RE4 Remake are not required to complete the game, but they can be extremely helpful in locating hidden treasures. The maps are available for trade-in at the Merchant for spinels, and once you obtain a region’s treasure map, the labeled treasures will appear on your world map.

What is the most valuable treasure in RE4?

The most valuable treasure in the RE4 Remake is the Elegant Crown. The ideal combination for the Elegant Crown is a Duo and Trio configuration with two yellow gems and three red beryls. This combination increases the crown’s price to 108,000 Ptas., making it the most expensive item in the game.

Does the treasure map carry over in RE4 remake?

Yes, the treasure map carries over in the RE4 Remake. When you begin a New Game+ run after killing the final boss, almost everything carries over except for key items and side quest progress. This means that you get to keep all your weapons, upgraded gear, ammo, healing items, and the treasures that you have already obtained.

What is the treasure map expansion RE4?

The treasure map expansion in the RE4 Remake allows additional treasures to be placed throughout the game. Some of these treasures can only be obtained by purchasing the expansion. The additional treasures’ locations can be confirmed in your map in the main story. Please note that this content is also included as part of the Extra DLC Pack.

Can you clean dirty treasures in RE4?

Unfortunately, once a treasure falls into swamps or wells in RE4, its value drops significantly, and there is no way to clean them or reverse the damage. The only way to keep treasures clean is to avoid dropping them into contaminated waters in the first place.

Where is Auridon Treasure Map 4?

In The Elder Scrolls Online, Auridon Treasure Map 4 can be found in the middle part of the Auridon map on the east coast. Start from Quendeluun Wayshrine and head towards the east coast. The treasure map is located directly below the boss area called Heretic’s Summons.

Where do I find the Hunters Kingdom treasure?

The Hunters Kingdom treasure can be found in Veithurgard in God of War. At the entrance to the castle, where you have to smash the runes to open the door, look for a pile of stones by the lore marker. Dig up the treasure, which is right behind the fallen statue head on the ground.
