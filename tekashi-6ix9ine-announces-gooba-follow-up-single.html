Tekashi 6ix9ine Announces &quot;Gooba&quot; Follow-Up Single

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Prior to being known as a snitch, Tekashi 6ix9ine was gloating about how successful he was on the Billboard Hot 100. Every time he released a new song, he would end up charting, which is not something that comes easy to most artists. After his prison release, he dropped his return single "Gooba" and, due to the public's curiosity, it has a decent chance of debuting at #1 on the chart. He's got some fierce competition in Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande though.

Giving some added incentive to his supporters, Tekashi 6ix9ine informed people that if the track starts off at the top of the charts, he will release a follow-up single.

Taking to social media, 6ix9ine asked his fans to purchase "Gooba" on iTunes, crossing his fingers for 10,000 additional units. "Ill drop a new song next week," he added.

On Twitter, he claimed that the follow-up video would only be released if his fans are successful in getting the track to a #1 debut. 

Tekashi 6ix9ine has spent much of his time post-release on his trolling tip, coming after everyone from Meek Mill and YG to 50 Cent and Rich The Kid. He continues to antagonize his fellow rappers on his quest to become the most popular artist in the world.

Do you think he'll be lucky enough to return on top?
