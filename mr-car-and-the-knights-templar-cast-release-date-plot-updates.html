The novel is part of a series featuring the adventures of Tomasz N.N., a young journalist and amateur detective who travels around Poland in his unusual car, nicknamed Mr. Car. The car is inherited from his uncle-inventor and has incredible capabilities, such as running at a speed of 280 km per hour, serving as a motor boat, and having various gadgets and weapons.

In this movie adaptation, Tomasz (played by Mateusz Janicki) finds an ancient Templar cross in an art museum and decides to investigate its secrets. Together, they embark on a quest that leads them to the Teutonic castles and the headquarters of the Templars in Poland, where they face dangers, puzzles, and enemies.
