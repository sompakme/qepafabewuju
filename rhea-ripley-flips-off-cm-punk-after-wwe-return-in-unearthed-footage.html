Rhea Ripley Flips Off CM Punk After WWE Return in Unearthed Footage

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Rhea Ripley is undoubtedly one of the top female WWE Superstars right now, as her stock continues to rise with each and every passing week. She had solid match at Survivor Series and was present for CM Punk’s return at the end as well. That said, Ripley was spotted taunting CM Punk after his return in unearthed footage.

As Survivor Series in Chicago was about to end, WWE gave fans the shock of a lifetime as CM Punk made his electric return to the company that he once called home. Naturally, this was an unforgettable moment for many fans and even wrestlers.

As seen in footage that Ringside News has obtained, Rhea Ripley flipped the bird at CM Punk while he made his return. In addition to that, Punk taunted Punk by mimicking his signature hand gesture before making weird faces.

Rhea Ripley isn’t the only WWE Superstar who had a negative reaction to CM Punk’s return, as Seth Rollins was absolutely furious. We will have to wait and see what WWE has planned for Punk now that he is back in the fold for good.
