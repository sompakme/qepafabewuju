Who is Savannah Chrisley Dating?

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Savannah Chrisley, the popular TV host, and actor has been making headlines recently because of an intriguing Instagram post. People are curious about her dating life and pregnancy rumors. In this article, we’ll delve into her relationship status and address the pregnancy speculation.

Savannah Chrisley – A Brief Introduction

Savannah Chrisley, born on August 11, 1997, has made a name for herself in the world of reality TV. She’s not only a beauty queen, having been crowned Miss Tennessee Teen U.S.A, but she’s also ventured into the world of fashion with her clothing line for H.S.N. Moreover, she’s been pursuing her academic goals by working towards a music degree. Her life has been showcased in the reality show “Chrisley Knows Best” since its first season.

Savannah’s Current Relationship Status

As of now, Savannah Chrisley isn’t dating anyone. However, before this, she had a secret relationship with Nate Smith. Recently, in her podcast, “Unlocked,” she revealed her past romance with country star Nate Smith, explaining that they had a great time together. Unfortunately, due to their busy lives and the challenges they faced, they decided to slow things down.

The Reasons Behind the Breakup

Savannah mentioned that her parents’ prison sentences had caused her a lot of stress. On the other hand, Nate’s music career was soaring, with sold-out stadium shows. She acknowledged that he needed someone who could fully enjoy his success and support him.

Addressing Pregnancy Rumors

Contrary to recent rumors, Savannah Chrisley is not pregnant. The rumors emerged after she posted a picture on Instagram holding a growing baby bump. In the photo, Savannah wore a big smile on her face, appearing quite happy. Her Instagram comment, written in all capital letters and ending with a heart-eye emoji, expressed her excitement while she stood with two close friends. Although it seemed like she was at a baby shower in the picture, the caption provided little information.

Conclusion

Savannah Chrisley, a reality TV star and fashion entrepreneur, has been a hot topic lately due to her Instagram post about her dating life and the surrounding pregnancy rumors. While she previously had a relationship with country star Nate Smith, they decided to take a step back due to various challenges. Despite their breakup in 2020, Savannah and Nic Kerdiles have rekindled their romance, keeping it out of the public eye.
