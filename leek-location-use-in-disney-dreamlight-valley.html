Leek Location &amp; Use In Disney Dreamlight Valley

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Looking to add more vegetables to your Disney Dreamlight Valley garden? You're in luck. Here is how to get Leek Seeds and Leeks and use them in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

First Published September 20, 2022, 18:53

Leeks, like Tomatoes, are one of the vegetables in Disney Dreamlight Valley that aren't unlocked at the very beginning of the game; you'll have to do some work in order to add Leeks to your garden collection.

Thankfully, though, once you've unlocked a certain area, they become rather easy to obtain.

Here is how to get Leeks in Disney Dreamlight Valley so you can get some of these useful ingredients for yourself.

Where To Find Leeks In Disney Dreamlight Valley

If you're looking for the best Leek location in Disney Dreamlight Valley, there's actually only one place you can get Leek seeds: The Forgotten Lands.

You will need to make your way there and complete tasks and quests, including clearing Night Thorns, for a chance to get Leek Seeds. Leek Seeds are a randomly spawned reward, so you may have to complete several tasks to earn them - but rest assured you'll find some eventually.

Leek Seeds are not available at Goofy's Stall, so, unfortunately, you won't be able to purchase them there; you'll have to put the work in. 

Planting the Leek seeds in your garden will result in you growing some Leeks, which have various uses around the valley in delicious recipes for you and your friends.

Uses Of Leek In Disney Dreamlight Valley

As we mentioned, Leeks have numerous uses in Disney Dreamlight Valley. Here are a few of the ways you can use Leek in recipes:

  • Potato Leek Soup - Leek, Potato, Milk, Onion, Garlic
  • Leek Soup - Leek

Leek is one of the only vegetables that cannot be used on its own to make the one-star recipe Salad, which requires any one vegetable to create it. This is because using one Leek creates Leek Soup instead, and therefore cannot create Salad.

Now you know where to find Leek in Disney Dreamlight Valley, as well as what it can be used for.
